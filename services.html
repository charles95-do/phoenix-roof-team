Our Roofing Services

Everything your roof needs — built for the Arizona desert, from quick repairs to full replacements.

Tile Roof Repair

Clay and concrete tile roofs are the standard across Phoenix — and they take a beating from the desert sun, monsoon winds, and thermal cycling. Cracked, broken, or displaced tiles leave your underlayment exposed to UV damage and leaks. We specialize in tile roof repair and replacement, matching your existing tiles for a seamless look. Whether it's a few broken tiles from a storm or widespread cracking from years of 115°F summers, we get it fixed fast and right.

  • Cracked, broken, and displaced tile replacement
  • Clay and concrete tile matching
  • Underlayment repair and replacement
  • Ridge cap and hip tile re-setting
  • Flashing repair around vents and penetrations
  • Emergency tile roof repair (same-day available)

Roof Replacement

When repairs aren't enough, it's time for a full replacement. Phoenix roofs endure some of the harshest conditions in the country — extreme UV radiation, 150°F+ surface temperatures, and sudden monsoon downpours. We do complete tear-off and replacement with heat-resistant materials specifically designed for the Arizona climate. Most residential roofs completed in 2-4 days with minimal disruption to your household.

  • Full tear-off and disposal of old roofing
  • Concrete and clay tile installation
  • Architectural shingle systems (heat-rated)
  • Flat roof replacement (foam, TPO, modified bitumen)
  • High-temp synthetic underlayment
  • New flashing, drip edge, and ventilation
  • Manufacturer warranty + workmanship guarantee

Monsoon Storm Damage

Arizona's monsoon season brings haboobs, microburst winds exceeding 70 mph, hail, and torrential rain. When your roof takes a hit, we respond fast — same-day emergency tarping to stop the damage, followed by a full assessment and restoration. We work directly with your insurance company, meet with your adjuster on-site, and handle the entire claims process so you can focus on your family, not paperwork.

  • Emergency tarping and temporary weatherproofing
  • Wind, hail, and microburst damage assessment
  • Insurance claim assistance (we meet with your adjuster)
  • Full photo documentation for your claim
  • Debris removal and cleanup
  • Complete storm damage restoration

Cool Roof Coatings

Phoenix summers push rooftop temperatures past 170°F, and all that heat transfers straight into your home. Reflective cool roof coatings bounce sunlight away instead of absorbing it, reducing your roof surface temperature by up to 50°F and cutting cooling costs by 20-30%. It's one of the smartest investments a Phoenix homeowner can make — you'll feel the difference the first day, and your energy bills will prove it every month.

  • Elastomeric reflective coatings
  • Silicone roof coatings for flat roofs
  • Acrylic coatings for tile and shingle roofs
  • UV-resistant, Energy Star-rated products
  • Extends roof life by 10-15 years
  • Reduces cooling costs by up to 30%

Flat Roof & Foam Roofing

Flat and low-slope roofs are extremely common across the Valley — on commercial buildings, patio covers, and many residential homes. We install and repair spray polyurethane foam (SPF), TPO single-ply membrane, and modified bitumen systems. Foam roofing is especially popular in Phoenix because it provides seamless waterproofing, superior insulation, and reflects heat — all in one system. Perfect for the desert.

  • Spray polyurethane foam (SPF) roofing
  • TPO single-ply membrane installation
  • Modified bitumen roofing
  • Flat roof leak repair and patching
  • Foam roof re-coating and maintenance
  • Commercial and residential flat roof systems
