About Phoenix Roof Team

Family-owned, Valley-raised roofing experts who know Arizona heat inside and out.

Built for the Desert

Phoenix Roof Team is a family-owned roofing company that's been protecting Valley homes for over 15 years. We started with a simple belief: Phoenix homeowners deserve a roofer who actually understands the desert — the 115°F summers, the monsoon chaos, the UV that eats through cheap materials in a few years. That's us.

We're not a franchise. We're not a national chain sending subcontractors who've never set foot in Arizona. We're a licensed ROC contractor with a crew that lives and works right here in the Valley. When you call us, you talk to real people who know your neighborhood, your roof style, and exactly what the Arizona climate does to it.

From emergency monsoon repairs to full tile roof replacements and energy-saving cool roof coatings — we treat every job with the same care and craftsmanship, whether it's a single cracked tile or a complete re-roof. That's how we've built our reputation across the Valley, and it's how we plan to keep it.
